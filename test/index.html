Freedom English Academy (FEA) is the leading provider of free, non-governmental, intensive English training and personal development in India. We serve thousands of students daily, providing them with hard and soft skills to enable them to access professional jobs and improve their quality of life.

Our beliefs

We believe that with effective tools and training, all people have the strength to improve their lives and the lives of those in their community. To that end, we provide our students with an intensive, one-year learning program. Our program empowers our graduates to access jobs and opportunities that would otherwise be unavailable to them, enabling them to improve their lives and the lives of their families and communities.

Our program

The program we offer students is free of charge. Our students attend a one hour and forty-five minute class six days a week, for the program duration of 52 weeks. Our class size is limited to 20 students. Our coaching centers are currently within a 100-mile radius of our staff training facility in New Delhi. We are expanding our reach by making our technology and intellectual property freely available for anyone to use anywhere in the developing world.

Our dynamic teaching method uses a hybrid of computer-based training, lectures, seminars, workbooks, peer-to-peer interaction and game-based learning. We use a cloud-based learning management system (LMS) to track the progress of individual students on a daily basis. Analysis of this data provides insights for improving our program, as well as providing useful information for researchers.

Academic Research &
Collaboration

FEA actively partners with academics that have a hypothesis and wish to formulate, design, and test studies using our teaching sites. Researchers benefit from the one-year duration of our programs, and our robust data collection practices. As an entrepreneur-led NGO, we are passionate about growth, improvement, and have a willingness to question the status quo.

As a learning and sharing organization, we deliberately do not copyright our course material and teaching methods. We are happy to share all our resources with anybody, worldwide.

We offer to host and train staff of other organizations that wish to use our curriculum and methods.
